\n
A viewer and processing tool for raw X-nuclei MRSI data. \n
- View a mutli-dimensional data volume per specific dimension attribute. \n
- Merge MRI and MRSI space for localization.\n
- Process data: from k-space to individual spectra.\n
- Calculate maps or specific spectrum properties.\n
- Edit and save a single spectrum from bulk data.\n
\n
Supports dat files (Siemens), list/data files (Philips), \n
spar/sdat files (Philips), dicom files (Siemens/Philips) and \n
par/rec files (Philips).\n
